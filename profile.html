History of St. Denis Academy

Saint Denis Academy was build by our founder, Arthur D. Redwood in 1923 at Hereford, UK. His vision is to build an Academy to prepare our students both pyschologicaly and physicaly for the world of adultry. In the span of 100 years, Saint Denis Academy rarely had any incident. In 1985, a school fire erupted in the cafeteria kitchen. The fire was small and none of our students are harmed by the incident. After the incident, we made sure it wont happen again in our Academy.

Motto, Vision, and Mission


  • Motto : "Prepare For The Future!"

  • Vision : The realization of students who excel in academic, non-academic, honest, disciplined and noble morals

  • Mission :
    • Carry out active, innovative, creative, effective and fun learning
    • Facilitate the development of students' potential in academic and non academic fields
    • Cultivate a person who has an honest, disciplined, and noble character.
    • Increase faith and piety through religious refraction
